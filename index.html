ABOUT

Hey there. This is weird speaking up. Talking to all you normal people. You might be unique in your own sense but me, well I am weird. Why do I say so? Maybe, because I wear fancy jewellery at all times, maybe because I am gay, maybe because I have a radical taste in music, or maybe because I am a mix of many things that 'normal' people disapprove of. I am what you can't be, what you might not like. I am a culture unlike you. I am counter-culture. And you have to know me, to feel me.

Get ready people. This Oasis, you are going to experience emotions which you never thought you would.

Welcome aboard to a journey through a lifestyle of unconventionality. Oasis'18 - "The Far Out Fest" from 27 th to 31 st October'18.
